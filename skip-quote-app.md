# SKIP — Daily motivational quote app

**Input:**

> I want to build an AI app that generates a motivational quote for me every
> morning, paired with a scenic photo.

This is the case that broke the first version of the scoring prompt. Before the
fix it scored 4/4/5 and came back **BUILD**.

Note what the report does *not* contain: no MVP, no 7-day plan, no launch post.
A tool that tells you not to build something, and then hands you a build plan,
is not really telling you not to build it.

---

## 1. Verdict

SKIP

## 2. Target User

Individuals seeking daily motivation or inspiration, particularly busy
professionals or students.

## 3. Core Problem

Lack of daily motivation or inspiration to start the day on a positive note.

## 4. Evaluation Scores

- **User Clarity Score:** 3
  *Reason:* The target user is broad, encompassing busy professionals and
  students, but lacks specificity regarding their particular needs or contexts
  for motivation.

- **Problem Strength Score:** 2
  *Reason:* While users may enjoy daily motivation, they often rely on existing
  free resources like apps or social media that provide similar content,
  indicating the problem is mild and more of a nice-to-have.

- **MVP Feasibility Score:** 4
  *Reason:* The MVP can be developed within 7 days with basic functionality for
  quote generation and photo pairing, along with simple customization options,
  without complex external data.

## 5. Why This Is Not Worth Building

The market for motivational quotes is saturated with free resources available on
social media and existing apps. The problem is not pressing enough to warrant
development, as users can easily access similar content without needing a new app.

## Alternative Direction

No alternative recommended. This problem is already solved well enough by
existing free options.
